ABOUT OUR PROJECT

How It Works

AeroGuard is an system designed to protect wildlife. It helps spot unusual activities in forests and alerts authorities in real time to stop poaching and other threats. With smart tracking and monitoring, AeroGuard makes it easier to safeguard endangered animals and support conservation efforts.

  • Monitor wildlife areas from above.
  • Advanced image recognition to detect poaching threats.
  • Notify authorities.
  • Provide locations for quick response.
  • Improve wildlife protection strategies.
  • Detect threats even in low-light conditions.

Features

Our AeroGuard system is equipped with advanced AI technology to ensure efficient and effective wildlife anomaly detection.
Below are the key features that make our system unique:

  • Live Detect

  • Alert

  • Location

AI-powered real-time anomaly detection.

This feature enables the system to monitor wildlife areas in real-time, identifying threats such as poachers or distressed animals. With live video processing, the system can immediately detect suspicious movements and unauthorized human presence.

  • Identifies unusual movement patterns in wildlife reserves.
  • Uses YOLO and Pytorch to detect unauthorized human presence.
  • Processes live video feeds from drones for immediate analysis.

Automated emergency notifications upon threat detection.

Whenever a potential threat is detected, AeroGuard sends alerts to the Base Station. These alerts contain critical details, including Detected Class, Date & Time and Location Coordinates, ensuring rapid response.

  • Notifies wildlife authorities of suspicious activities.
  • Sends alerts via SMS.
  • Ensures rapid response to potential poaching incidents.

Integrated location tracking for precise monitoring and rapid response.

Location tracking allows authorities to locate threats. This feature is essential for tracking Fire, Vehicles, identifying poaching hotspots, and guiding security teams to the location of incidents.

  • Provides real-time location tracking of detected threats.
  • Guides security teams to coordinates for intervention.
  • Logs location data for future analysis and pattern recognition.
